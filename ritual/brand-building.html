We worked with Ritual to build a new kind of health technology company

Ritual is a health technology company committed to creating the purest, most effective vitamin formulations, and sharing every ingredient, source & scientist that made their vitamin a reality. Their debut product—an essential daily vitamin—boasts completely traceable and transparent ingredients so you know exactly what you’re swallowing.

Teaching good habits

The hardest challenge in a subscription vitamin model is retention: and specifically, helping those new to vitamins remember to take them every day and form the habit. To help with retention and building good habits we tested a few methods and mini-campaigns to learn what works.

Sticker calendar

As a retention strategy (and for a daily dose of fun), we designed a sticker calendar and placed them into orders so customers could have a rewards-driven method to track their new habit.
